Custom Benches by Slater

Deigned by You. Built by Us.

You pick the features, size, color, and type of bench that you want. We send you all of the pieces that you need and instructions to build your custom bench.

Weather Proof

All of our benches are made out of weater resistant wood. Aff of the finished wood makes the wood both waterproof and UV proof. All hardware included in the kit is also waterproof and rust-resistant.

Durrable

All of our benches come with a 6 year warrenty which includes both the wood and the hardware included in the kits. Although our benches come with a 6 month warrenty they are designed to last for many many years.

Easy to Design

Our easy to use website lets you choose the specific options that you want. This allows you to design a bench that fits your needs and matches the area you plan to put it in.

Easy to Build

All of our kits are sent with easy to use instructions and hardware for easy construction. All you will need to put it together is a drill with a phillips bit.

FAQ
You've got questions, we've got answers

What tools will I need in order to assemble my bench?

You will need a drill with a bit in order to screw inn all of the included screws

Can I choose colors other than those that are on the website?

You are not able to choose colors other than those that are on our website. Instead order the unfinished wood option and paint or stain it yourself.

Is there a warrenty on the benches?

We provide a 6 month warrenty on all of out bench hardware and wood. If a piece breaks, email us with a picture of the damage and a description of the broken part and we will mail you a replacement parts as soon as possible.
